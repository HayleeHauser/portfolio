My Contributions

Features I Built:

  • User Authentication: Implemented login and signup.
  • Password Management: Password reset and reset confirmation.
  • Movie Search with Filters: Enabled search by sort, year, genre, and language.
  • Featured Film Section: Highlighted a recommended movie on the homepage.
  • Top Review Section: Highlighted top reviews at the bottom of the film details page.
  • User Settings: Settings page for updating preferences and account information.
  • Social Features: Friends system including following, followers, and friend requests.
